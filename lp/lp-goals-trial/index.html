What's underneath.

The SMART-goal trap.

SMART optimizes for measurability, not for the behaviors that produce the outcome — it doesn't model the path. Without the path, the goal becomes wishful.

Rarely shows up alone — co-occurs with status asymmetry and decision avoidance.

We change the loop, not the symptom.
